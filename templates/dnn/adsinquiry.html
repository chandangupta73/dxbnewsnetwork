Voices of UAE

Celebrating Achievers Across Fields

The campaign aims to highlight and celebrate the inspiring achievements of individuals from diverse fields and backgrounds in the UAE.

Eligibility

This campaign is open to individuals currently residing in the UAE from all fields, including but not limited to:

  • Law, Arts, Culture, History, Education, Performing Arts (dance, acting, theatre)
  • Literature (writers, lyricists, poets)
  • Visual Arts (painters, sculptors, photographers)
  • Social Media Influencers, Entrepreneurs, Innovators, and Students

No restrictions on age, gender, nationality, or socio-economic background.

Registration

A non-refundable registration fee of AED 220 will be required, payable to Markazzia Solutions LLC-FZ.

Participants must provide a deposit slip as proof of payment along with their submissions.

Verification Process

Submissions will undergo basic verification by DXB News Network to ensure authenticity. Participants must provide supporting documents or evidence (e.g., certificates, work samples, testimonials) for their claims.

Campaign Categories

1. Young Children (Ages 5–12)

  • Creative Arts: Drawing, Painting, Craftwork
  • Performing Arts: Dance, Acting/Theater
  • Sports: Emerging Athletes
  • Academic Achievements: Science Projects
  • Social Contributions: Environmental Initiatives

2. Teenagers (Ages 13–19)

  • Academic Excellence: Innovators in STEM
  • Creative Fields: Writers, Poets, Lyricists
  • Performing Arts: Dance, Drama
  • Sports: State/National Level Athletes
  • Social Media: Teen Influencers

3. Young Adults (Ages 20–35)

  • Career Growth: Entrepreneurs, Innovators
  • Cultural Impact: Artists, Writers
  • Technology and Innovation: App Developers
  • Entertainment and Media: Actors, Social Media Influencers

4. Adults (Ages 36–60)

  • Professional Excellence: Doctors, Lawyers, Engineers
  • Educational Impact: Teachers, Mentors
  • Creative Fields: Published Authors
  • Social Work and Leadership: Women’s Rights Advocates

5. Senior Citizens (Ages 60 and Above)

  • Legacy and Contributions: Lifetime Achievements in Arts
  • Creative Pursuits: Senior Writers, Poets
  • Community Service: Volunteers and Leaders

Cross-Cross_Sector Categories

  • Science and Technology: Innovators in AI, Blockchain
  • Entrepreneurship: Founders of Startups
  • Health and Wellness: Fitness Coaches, Medical Professionals
  • Civic Engagement: Local Heroes in Community Service
